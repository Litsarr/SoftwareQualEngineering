Built in the Islands.
For Islanders.

Islander Philippines was founded by the late KP Yao, a
Filipino-Chinese businessman, in 1985. The footwear brand
was initially produced and marketed by Rubberworld Phils.
Inc in Novaliches, Quezon City. Later on, several local factory
facilities were tapped to manufacture Islander products.
